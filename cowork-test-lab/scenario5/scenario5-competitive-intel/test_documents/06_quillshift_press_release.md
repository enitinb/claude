## Quillshift launches "Expense Pilot," its first vertical AI agent for expense reporting

*New product, in beta from today, ingests receipts from email, drive, or photo and produces a policy-checked expense report in minutes.*

---

**San Francisco — May 6, 2026 —** Quillshift, the AI document workflow platform, today announced **Expense Pilot**, a purpose-built AI agent for end-to-end expense reporting.

Expense Pilot ingests receipts from any source — forwarded email, file drive, mobile photo upload — extracts line items, classifies each against the customer's corporate expense policy, flags policy exceptions with the rule that was triggered, and produces a submission-ready report.

"Expense reporting is the single most common workflow on our platform," said CEO Mara Velasquez. "Expense Pilot takes what was a generic agent flow and replaces it with software that knows what an expense report is supposed to look like."

Expense Pilot enters beta today for **Team-plan and above customers**. General availability is expected in **August 2026**.

### Key features

- **Policy-aware classification.** Customers upload a written policy document; Expense Pilot applies it.
- **Multi-source ingestion.** Pull receipts from Gmail, Outlook, Google Drive, OneDrive, or mobile.
- **Native integration with NetSuite, QuickBooks, and Xero** for one-click submission.
- **Audit trail.** Every classification and every flagged exception is logged with the rule that was applied.
