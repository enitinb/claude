PROSPECT CALL TRANSCRIPT
========================
Date: 2026-04-30
Account: Hartwell Industries (fictional, mid-market manufacturer, ~140 employees)
Stage: Evaluation — head-to-head with Quillshift
Attendees: Jordan Reyes (us), Devran Kaya (prospect, IT Director)

JORDAN: What does your CFO care about most?

JORDAN: Which integrations are non-negotiable for you?

JORDAN: NetSuite, Slack, and Microsoft 365 we cover. The homegrown PO system would be a custom build — I can scope that.

DEVRAN: One more thing — their support reputation worries me a little. I read some reviews about slow response times. But the product itself looked polished in the trial.

JORDAN: We can pull together customer references on support if that helps.

DEVRAN: Yeah, that'd be useful. The other thing I'll flag — my CFO read about Quillshift's funding round and that's actually a negative for him. He thinks they're going to push to monetize hard.

JORDAN: Understood. Let me follow up by Friday with the connector scoping and three customer references on support. Anything else?

DEVRAN: That works. We're moving fast on this one.
