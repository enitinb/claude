# Corporate Expense Policy — Cedarcrest (fictional)

*Effective 2026-01-01. All figures in USD.*

## 1. Reimbursable categories

| Category | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Airfare | Economy only. Premium economy permitted on flights longer than 6 hours. |
| Lodging | Up to $275/night in Tier-1 cities; $200/night elsewhere. |
| Ground transport | Rideshare, taxis, public transit, parking. |
| Meals — individual | Up to **$50 per person per meal**. |
| Meals — team / client | Up to **$60 per person per meal**, max 6 attendees per receipt; attendees must be listed. |
| Conference & event fees | Pre-approval required for fees over $1,000. |
| Coworking / meeting rooms | When traveling and no company office is available. |

## 2. Not reimbursable

- Personal items, clothing, gear (including outdoor or athletic equipment).
- Alcohol *except* one drink with a client meal (must be itemized).
- In-room minibar, movies, spa.
- Companion travel costs (spouse / family flights, meals, lodging).

## 3. Receipt requirements

- Itemized receipt required for any single expense **≥ $25**.
- For team meals, list all attendees and their affiliation.
- Submit within 30 days of the trip end date.

## 4. Tier-1 cities (for lodging cap)

San Francisco, New York, Boston, Seattle, Washington DC, London, Tokyo, Singapore.
